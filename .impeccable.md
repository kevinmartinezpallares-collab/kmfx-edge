## Design Context

### Users
KMFX Edge is used by funded-account and MT5 traders who need a fast operational dashboard for account health, risk limits, journal review, funding rules, and execution discipline. Mobile use is mostly quick-check and intervention-oriented: glance at account status, move between key sections, and inspect risk without losing context.

### Brand Personality
Precise, disciplined, and quietly premium. The interface should feel closer to Apple HIG, shadcn, and Base UI than to a marketing dashboard: direct, structured, calm, touch-friendly, and confident.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first operational app with restrained surfaces, crisp hierarchy, sidebar-led navigation, compact controls, and no bottom tab bar. Mobile should feel like the dashboard shell adapted to touch: an off-canvas sidebar that can be hidden, with content spacing adjusted for small screens.

### Design Principles
- Preserve the dashboard information architecture across desktop and mobile.
- Use a collapsible sidebar/drawer instead of duplicating navigation in a bottom bar.
- Keep mobile spacing compact but not cramped, with 44px touch targets where interaction matters.
- Prefer clear structural rhythm, minimal decoration, and visible account/risk context.
- Avoid hiding core sections on mobile; navigation should reveal the same app surface.
